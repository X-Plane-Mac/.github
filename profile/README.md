## About X-Plane  

**X-Plane** is a world-class **flight simulator** for macOS, designed for aviation enthusiasts, pilots-in-training, and gamers who want a realistic flying experience.  

Developed by **Laminar Research**, X-Plane features **ultra-realistic physics**, detailed 3D aircraft models, and **accurate world scenery** powered by real-world geographic data.  

Whether you're flying a small propeller plane, a modern jetliner, or a military fighter, X-Plane delivers **true-to-life flight dynamics**. It is used not only for entertainment but also for **professional pilot training and flight schools** worldwide.  

Optimized for **Apple Silicon Macs (M1, M2, M3)**, the simulator provides smooth performance and stunning visuals on modern macOS devices, including **MacBook Pro, Mac Studio, and Mac Mini**.
